Djerba Zarzis International Airport

The international airport of Djerba (IATA code: DJE • ICAO: DTTJ) serves the island of Djerba and more generally the entire south-eastern of Tunisia. Located 9 kilometers west of Houmt Souk, it is put into operation in 1970 to enhance the tourist appeal of Djerba. Covering an area of 295 hectares and a capacity of 4 million passengers a year, its activity is primarily related to the routing of tourists visiting Djerba. The terminal covers 130,000 m² on three levels.
